About the program

Connecting students with Google's ecosystem

The Google Student Ambassador Program selects passionate students at leading universities to act as on-campus representatives for Google. Ambassadors run events, organize workshops, and create opportunities for peers to engage with Google technologies.

The IIT Patna chapter is led by Sayan Dutta alongside a core team — with many more members contributing across different working groups throughout the academic year.
